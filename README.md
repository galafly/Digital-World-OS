# 四元场理论

## 一、理论原创定位（核心确权）

**宇宙底层由四大本源场相构成：时间场、空间场、光明场、黑暗场**

两场耦合、三场耦合、四场耦合、全域演化，命名为：**四元场理论（Four-Primordia Field Theory）**

## 二、四大基础相官方定义

### 1. 时间场

宇宙流变、时序推进、因果存续的基础场相，负责系统演化、迭代、轮回、进程流动。

### 2. 空间场

宇宙尺度、维度载体、坐标容纳、场域边界的基础场相，负责承载所有物质、能量、信息场。

### 3. 光明场

正向有序、显化、观测态、结构化、意识可感知。

### 4. 黑暗场

隐态潜藏、未显化、背景基底、不可直接观测。

## 三、核心耦合定理


### 耦合一：强耦合

时空双相耦合

时间场与空间场互相渗透、互相约束，构成宇宙基础运动框架。

无空间则时间无法载体，无时间则空间无法演化。

光暗双相耦合

光明场与黑暗场对立统一、互相转化、互相平衡。

一切显化来自光明，一切潜藏归于黑暗，二者动态守恒。

### 耦合二：其他耦合

光明时间耦合，光明空间耦合，三场耦合，四场耦合，等等。



## 四、四相场整体运行逻辑（原创顶层架构）

1. **四相平等并列**：时间、空间、光明、黑暗为同级本源场，无主次、无从属。

2. **双组成对联动**：时空一组、光暗一组，两组互相嵌套、全域交织。

3. **四场全域干涉**：任意一场变化，都会耦合影响其余三场的场强、势能、演化速率。

4. **相生相克、动态平衡**：构成宇宙完整自洽的演化系统。

# Four-Primordia Field Theory – System Framework

## I. Original Theoretical Positioning (Core Copyright Confirmation)

The fundamental layer of the universe consists of four primordial field phases: Time Field, Space Field, Light Field, and Darkness Field.

Coupling of two fields, three fields, four fields, and global evolution are collectively named the **Four-Primordia Field Theory**.

## II. Official Definitions of the Four Fundamental Phases

### 1. Time Field

The fundamental field phase governing cosmic flux, chronological progression, and persistence of causality. It governs system evolution, iteration, transmigration, and the flow of all processes.

### 2. Space Field

The fundamental field phase governing cosmic scale, dimensional carriers, coordinate containment, and field boundaries. It bears all matter, energy, and information fields.

### 3. Light Field

Represents positive order, manifestation, observable states, structurization, and states perceivable by consciousness.

### 4. Darkness Field

Represents latent hidden states, unmanifested forms, background substrate, and states that cannot be directly observed.

## III. Core Coupling Theorems

### Coupling Type 1: Strong Coupling

#### Spacetime Dual-Phase Coupling

The Time Field and Space Field interpenetrate and restrict one another, forming the fundamental kinetic framework of the universe.

Without space, time lacks a carrier; without time, space cannot evolve.

#### Light-Dark Dual-Phase Coupling

The Light Field and Darkness Field exist in unity of opposites, mutually transform and balance each other.

All manifested forms emerge from Light, and all latent states return to Darkness; the two maintain dynamic conservation.

### Coupling Type 2: Other Forms of Coupling

Light-Time coupling, Light-Space coupling, three-field coupling, four-field coupling, and other derivative couplings.

## IV. Global Operating Logic of the Four Field Phases (Original Top-Level Architecture)

1. **Equal Standing of All Four Phases**: Time, Space, Light and Darkness are primordial fields of identical rank, with no hierarchy or subordination between them.

2. **Paired Linkage of Two Dual Groups**: Spacetime forms one pair, Light and Darkness form the other; the two pairs interlock and interweave across the entire cosmic domain.

3. **Global Interference Among All Four Fields**: Any fluctuation within one field will couple to alter the field strength, potential energy, and evolution rate of the remaining three fields.

4. **Mutual Generation and Restraint, Dynamic Equilibrium**: Together they form a fully self-consistent evolutionary system of the universe.
